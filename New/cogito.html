CogITo Smart Journal is a scientific journal in the field of Computer Science published by the Faculty of Computer Science, Klabat University which is a member of CORIS (Cooperation Research Inter University) and IndoCEISS (Indonesian Computer Electronics and Instrumentation Support Society). Cogito Smart Journal is accredited by Sinta 2 (S2) and is indexed in various important indexing institutions, both national and international.

CogITo Smart Journal is published twice a year, namely every June and December. CogITo Smart Journal accepts various new and original manuscripts from research results, library reviews, and book references from the field of Computer Science and Informatics which may be written in English.
